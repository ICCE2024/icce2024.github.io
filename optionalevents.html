Optional Free Events



Guided Tour of the Ateneo Art Gallery

The Ateneo Art Gallery is the first museum of Philippine Modern Art. It is the University’s museum, now located in Areté. The Gallery is open from Mondays to Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. ICCE participants may visit the Gallery for free during these times by showing their ICCE name badge.

ICCE Participants who opted to join a guided tour through the ICCE Participation Form are reminded of the schedule:

  • Tour Schedule 1: Monday, November 25, 6:00 PM–8:00 PM
  • Tour Schedule 2: Wednesday, November 27, 6:00 PM –8:00 PM

The tour group will assemble at the Art Gallery Lobby of Areté to become acquainted with the museum's permanent collection and its special exhibits.

Please be aware that the Ateneo Art Gallery prohibits the entry of large bags, food, and drinks. Bags and drink bottles may be checked in with the reception desk at the lobby.



Nature Walk

The Ateneo Wild takes you on an early morning Nature Walk on November 26 (Tuesday) and be acquainted with the birds, trees, and other wildlife on campus. The group will meet up at the Ubuntu Space in Areté at 6:15 AM. The walk will end at 8:30 AM.

ICCE Participants who opted to join the Nature Walk through the ICCE Participation Form are reminded of the following:

  • Please be in neutral/muted colors and wear comfortable walking shoes.
  • Please bring an umbrella/hat and water for hydration.
  • The LOC will provide mosquito repellent.
  • Binoculars will be provided.


Sustainability Walk

The Ateneo Institute for Sustainability takes you on an early morning Sustainability Walk on November 27 (Wednesday). Participants will tour the campus, highlighting its sustainability infrastructures and biodiversity. The group will meet up at the Ubuntu Space in Areté at 6:15 AM. The walk will end at 8:30 AM.

ICCE Participants who opted to join the Sustainabilty Walk through the ICCE Participation Form are reminded of the following:

  • Please bring an umbrella/hat and water for hydration.
  • The LOC will provide mosquito repellent.
