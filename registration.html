Registration

  • Author Registration until 16 September 2024 (GMT)
    • authors of main conference papers
    • authors of workshop papers
    • authors of extended summaries
    • authors of work-in-progress posters
    • participants of ECW and DSC
    • Late registrations are welcome but your paper will not be included in the proceedings of the Conference.
    • A registrant may have at most two (2) papers published in the proceedings of the Conference.
  • Early Bird Registration until 13 October 2024 (GMT)
  • Regular Registration until 10 November 2024 (GMT)

Registration Cancellation Policy

Authors whose papers are to be published cannot cancel their registration but can nominate a representative to present their paper. There will be no refund on cancellation at ICCE 2024.

Registration Fee Inclusions

  • participation at the main conference and all pre-conference events
  • conference kits
  • meals and snacks for all five (5) days of the conference
  • Welcome Reception and Conference Banquet (for early bird and regular registration)
  • APSCE membership for 2024

You are an APSCE member if you registered for ICCE 2023. Please contact managingsecretary@apsce.net for any queries regarding APSCE membership.

Emerging Economies

Individuals from these emerging economies as defined by World Bank are qualified for a discounted rate.

  • Low-Income Economies
  • Lower-Middle Income Economies
  • Upper-Middle-Income Economies

Please note that the discounted rate is based on the country of the institution (or the main employer) that the registrant is affiliated to.
