Conference Program

Oral Presentation

Before the Session Begins
  • Please check the schedule of your presentation.
  • Check in with your session chair.
  • Introduce yourself and state the title of your paper.
During the Session
  • The session chair will begin by welcoming the audience.
  • The session chair will introduce the time keeper to the presenters.
  • Note the following time limits for the different types of presentations
    • Full Paper : 25 minutes + 5 minutes Q&A
    • Shortl Paper : 15 minutes + 5 minutes Q&A
    • Extended Summary : 10 minutes + 5 minutes Q&A
  • The time keeper will be provided with cards indicating how much time the speaker has left. The time keeper will signal the speaker when the speaker has 5 minutes, 2 minutes and 1 minute left. When time is up, the time keeper will hold up the WRAP UP card. If the speaker goes over time, the session chair will try to intervene.
  • After the presentation, the session chair will ask the audience for questions.
  • If the presenter goes over time, the session chair will reduce the time from the Question and Answer portion.
General Notes
  • Please bring your own computer for the presentation to prevent potential compatibility issues. The connection between the computer and the projector is via HDMI. A laptop will be available for participants' use, if necessary. Also please bring a USB memory stick (flash drive) with you with copies of your presentation (PowerPoint and PDF).
  • Please set up and test your presentation in the designated room prior to your session.

Poster Presentation

  • Format: width x height , portrait
  • The presentation time for each session is 60 minutes. Please check your presentation time in the programme.
  • Please include the title of the paper, the names and affiliations of the authors in the poster.
  • The contents of the poster should be clear and concise. Figures, tables and letters on the posters should be large and clear enough that they are readable from a distance. Letters in font size less than 1 cm should be avoided.
  • Electrical outlets will not be available.
  • Wi-Fi Internet connection will be provided.
  • Please post your poster before your presentation time.
